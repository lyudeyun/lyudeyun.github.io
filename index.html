About me

I am a second year PhD student in the Department of Information Science and Electrical Engineering, Kyushu University, under the guidance of Prof. Jianjun Zhao. My research mainly focuses on the quality assurance of AI-enabled Cyber-physical systems (CPS) based on falsification and verification.
