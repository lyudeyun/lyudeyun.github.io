About Me

I am currently a Research Fellow at National Institute of Informatics, working in the Trustworthy & Smart Software Engineering Lab led by Prof. Fuyuki ISHIKAWA. Before that, I received my PhD from Department of Information Science and Electrical Engineering (ISEE), Kyushu University. My supervisor was Prof. Jianjun Zhao, and I was co-advised by Prof. Paolo Arcaini and Prof. Zhenya Zhang. My research interests mainly focus on quality assurance of AI-enabled cyber-physical systems (AI-enabled CPSs).
